# Governance Rules

## Status

LOCKED

Derived from Research Constitution

---

# Rule 1 — Constitution Supremacy

The Research Principles Document is the highest authority of this repository.

No document may contradict the Constitution.

---

# Rule 2 — Traceability

Every research decision shall be traceable to:

* Research Principle(s)
* Research Decision(s)
* Research Document(s)

---

# Rule 3 — LOCKED Documents

Documents marked as LOCKED shall not be modified unless a methodological justification has been approved.

---

# Rule 4 — Change Management

Every methodological change shall:

1. update the Constitution if required;
2. update the Decision Register;
3. update affected R-documents;
4. update CHANGELOG.md.

---

# Rule 5 — Engineering Governance

Engineering shall implement capabilities.

Engineering shall not define research concepts.

---

# Rule 6 — Evaluation Governance

Evaluation shall validate capabilities.

Evaluation shall not validate software configuration alone.

---

# Rule 7 — Repository Integrity

Every repository artifact shall belong to exactly one category:

* Governance
* Research
* Engineering
* Evidence
* Archive

---

# Rule 8 — Version Control

Each completed governance document shall be committed separately.

One scientific milestone equals one Git commit.
